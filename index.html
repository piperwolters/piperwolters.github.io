About

I am currently a research engineer at the Allen Institute for AI where I am working on applying deep learning models to large amounts of remote sensing imagery, in an effort to make the information in public satellite imagery easily accessible to both researchers and non-ML experts. Prior to this, I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science at Western Washington University, where my research ranged from few-shot learning for audio classification and event detection, to emulating climate data with generative adversarial networks, to detecting archeological features from aerial lidar data.
